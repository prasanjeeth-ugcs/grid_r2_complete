# Camera Tips

- **Slow scroll** through charts to show detail
- **Pause 2-3 seconds** on key numbers (28.2, 1,775, 89.1)
- **Hover over** interactive elements to show tooltips
- **Click markers** on map to show popups
- **Use cursor highlights** to draw attention to important sections
- **Zoom in** on complex visualizations

---

# Audio Tips

- Keep voice clear and steady
- Pause briefly between sections
- Emphasize key numbers: "28.2", "1,775 similar incidents", "R² = 0.9522"
- Use professional tone - this is for Flipkart Grid judges
- Background music: subtle, professional (optional)
