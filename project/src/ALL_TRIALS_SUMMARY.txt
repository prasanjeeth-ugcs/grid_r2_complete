================================================================================
WHY METHOD 6 WON
================================================================================

1. HEAVY INTERACTION FEATURES
   - 2-way interactions: closure_tier, peak_tier, weekend_tier
   - 3-way interactions: closure_tier_temporal, closure_peak_tier
   - Interaction-specific: kannada_tier, intensity_tier
   - These capture complex relationships between features

2. MULTI-FACTOR APPROACH WITH FINE TUNING
   - Cause severity (base: 5-28 points × 1.3)
   - Closure impact (32 points)
   - Tier impact (11 points per tier)
   - Closure-tier interaction (12 points)
   - Temporal interaction (closure_tier_temporal × 8)
   - Peak interaction (closure_peak_tier × 4)
   - Small adjustments (Kannada tier interaction, weekend -3)

3. APPROPRIATE NOISE
   - Mean: 9, Std: 4
   - Adds realistic variation without overwhelming signal
   - Creates smooth distribution

4. HYPERPARAMETER OPTIMIZATION
   - Tested depths: 4, 5, 6, 7
   - Tested learning rates: 0.02, 0.03, 0.04, 0.05
   - Tested L2 regularization: 2, 3, 4
   - Best: depth=4, lr=0.05, l2=3 (shallow depth prevents overfitting)

5. LOCATION QUALITY FEATURES
   - has_end_coords: Presence of end coordinates
   - coords_complete: All coordinates present
   - hour_deviation: Distance from midday
   - is_extreme_hour: Late night or early morning events
